
# 🌌 Lumora

**A space where your inner vision becomes visible.**

Lumora is an AI-powered manifestation and visualization platform that transforms your thoughts, journals, and intentions into personalized visual inspiration. It combines journaling, AI, and curated imagery to help users design, see, and align with their ideal life.

---

## ✨ Overview

Lumora bridges the gap between **inner thoughts and external reality**.

Instead of manually searching for inspiration, users simply write their dreams, and Lumora’s AI transforms them into:

- Visual inspiration feeds
- AI-generated vision boards
- Improved manifestation journals
- Daily alignment experiences

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 📓 Manifestation Diary
- Write daily manifestation entries
- AI improves grammar, clarity, and tone
- Converts text into powerful present-tense affirmations
- Timeline view of past entries

---

### 🧠 AI Manifestation Engine
- Understands user intent, emotions, and goals
- Extracts key themes (e.g. freedom, creativity, wealth)
- Enhances writing with:
  - positivity
  - emotional depth
  - manifestation structure

---

### 🖼 AI Visual Feed
- Personalized inspiration feed
- Automatically generated from journal entries
- Displays:
  - lifestyle visuals
  - dream environments
  - aspirational imagery

---

### 📌 Vision Boards (AI-Assisted)
- Create boards manually or via AI
- Generate full boards from a single sentence
- Organize by:
  - Dream Life
  - Career
  - Home
  - Self Growth

---

### 🤖 AI Dream Builder
- Generate future lifestyle visuals
- Create “dream life” simulations
- Build life timelines:
  - Present → 1 Year → 5 Years → Ideal Life

---

### 🌅 Daily Ritual Mode
- Guided daily experience:
  1. Write manifestation
  2. AI improves it
  3. Visuals appear
  4. Affirmations reinforce mindset

---

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- Track real-life manifestation progress
- Mark achieved goals
- Visualize personal growth journey
